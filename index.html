About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a Ph.D. student in Statistics at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. My research focuses on non-parametric derivative estimation with penalized B-splines, including asymptotics and improved estimator development.

With experience in statistical consulting, data science, and R programming, I am passionate about applying statistical methods to solve real-world problems. My work spans machine learning, predictive modeling, and sustainable development research.

I am proficient in multiple programming languages including R, Python, SQL, and LaTeX, with expertise in database management and statistical analysis.
